ABOUT US

Rescue TAILS: Taking Animals Into Loved & Sheltered was founded with a simple yet powerful goal: to provide loving homes for animals in need and to promote the importance of adoption over purchasing pets. We believe that every animal, regardless of their past, deserves a second chance at life, filled with love, care, and security. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce pet homelessness, raise awareness about responsible pet ownership, and connect compassionate adopters with animals who are eager to find their forever homes.

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. We strive to create a safe space for them to heal and thrive, offering each animal the love and care they deserve. By promoting adoption, we aim to decrease the number of animals in shelters and reduce the cycle of overpopulation. We envision a world where every animal has a loving home and where pet homelessness is a thing of the past. Through community engagement, education, and adoption initiatives, we aspire to foster a society that values compassion and care for all animals. We see a future where rescue shelters are no longer overcrowded, and every pet can live a happy, healthy life with a family that cherishes them.

Our Purpose is to give animals a second chance at life. Rescue Tails is a platform dedicated to showcasing the animals in our care, raising awareness about the joys and responsibilities of pet adoption, and making it easy for individuals and families to find their perfect pet. Whether you’re looking to adopt, donate, or simply learn more about how you can help, our website serves as a bridge between these wonderful animals and the loving homes they deserve. Through Rescue Tails, we not only save animals but also inspire a culture of kindness and responsible pet ownership.
